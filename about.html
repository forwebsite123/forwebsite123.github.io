Behind the pixels
On making this

I've never been comfortable with platforms that ask you to perform yourself in public — the optimized caption, the curated grid, the quiet pressure to post something because it's been too long since the last one. This site is a reaction to all of that. It doesn't have a follower count. It doesn't refresh. It just sits here, accumulating traces.

The sections are organized around things I actually do: places I've been, activities that have become habits, fragments I couldn't throw away, and immersive experiences that are hard to describe but impossible to forget. Nothing here is meant to be impressive. It's just what I wanted to keep.

"做一颗独立的树，而不是攀附在树上的藤。"

Where it came from

This site was a birthday gift. My 22nd. A friend built it for me

The domain still spells out my name. Some gifts you keep even after everything else is gone.
